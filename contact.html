Frequently Asked Questions

What's your typical turnaround time?

Standard projects take 3-5 business days. Rush services (24-48 hour turnaround) are available for an additional fee.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept all major video formats including MP4, MOV, AVI, and ProRes. For large files, we recommend using WeTransfer or Google Drive.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes! We include 2 rounds of revisions in all our packages. Additional revisions are available at an hourly rate.
